Jaipur

Rajasthan's palaces and forts are large in number, its architectures are the monuments today. Each architecture has its own history and grandeur in Rajasthan. Hawa Mahal was built by Rajput Sawai Pratap Singh in 1799. These interesting facts about Hawa Mahal will depict its elegance and the reason behind such a design. The palace is constructed with the red and pink sandstone, has a blend of Mughal and Rajasthani style of architecture. The artistic carvings and the structure of Hawal Mahal stands in the middle of the Jaipur in its elegance. Hawa Mahal is a unique and popular tourist place that should not be missed in Rajasthan.

Assam

Assam is mostly famous for tea gardens, one-horned rhinoceros, Digboi oil refinery, Bihu festival, Assam silk, Bhoot Jolokia, Kamakhya temple, Majuli island, Umananda Island, National Parks, and Wildlife Sanctuaries, Brahmaputra river, Ahom monuments, and many more. It is also famous because of its historical glories, diverse cultures, world heritage sites and outstanding diversity of various tribes and people.
